HOW TO MAKE MAGGIE

INGREDIENTS

  • ONION
  • TOMATO
  • MAGGIE
  • OIL OR GHEE

METHOD

  • Take a pan and put some oil in it.

  • When it get heated completely, add chopped onions and cook until it turns reddish.

  • After that add tomato and cooked it.

  • Now add 11/2cup water

  • Add maggie and its masala and boiled it for 2 min.

  • After 2 min turn off the flame.

  • Served it with kectup, and enjoyed it :)))
